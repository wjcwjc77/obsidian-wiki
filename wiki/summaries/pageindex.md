## Core points

- PageIndex argues that relevance requires reasoning rather than similarity alone.
- It builds a hierarchical table-of-contents-style index and performs tree-search retrieval over that structure.
- The design emphasizes explainability, traceability, and page-level grounding.

## Why it matters

This is an important counterpoint to vector-database-heavy RAG designs and fits the wiki's emphasis on inspectable knowledge structures.
