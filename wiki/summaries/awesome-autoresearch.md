## Core points

- AutoResearch is framed as an iterative keep-or-revert optimization loop over a measurable target.
- The curated list emphasizes optimization traces, not just final outcomes.
- The pattern has been adapted to model training, kernels, prompt engineering, forecasting, RL, and more.

## Why it matters

This source captures a reusable self-improvement pattern for agents rather than a single product.
