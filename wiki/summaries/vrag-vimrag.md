## Core points

- VRAG and VimRAG extend retrieval-augmented reasoning into multimodal settings across text, images, and video.
- The framework uses multimodal memory graphs and graph-guided policy optimization.
- The project includes retriever building, APIs, demos, and reinforcement-learning-based training support.

## Why it matters

This source widens the corpus from text-only retrieval toward richer multimodal agent memory and search workflows.
