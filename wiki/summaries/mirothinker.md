## Core points

- MiroThinker is a deep research agent oriented toward research and prediction tasks.
- The project highlights strong performance on BrowseComp-style benchmarks.
- Its roadmap includes report generation, sharing, and support for broader document uploads.

## Why it matters

It is the clearest deep-research system in the current corpus and anchors the benchmark side of research agents.
