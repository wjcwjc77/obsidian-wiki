# Software Team Economics: Flying Blind

- Source: [[raw/sources/blogs/The Economics of Software Teams Why Most Engineering Organizations Are Flying Blind|The Economics of Software Teams]]
- Topics: [[wiki/topics/deep-research-and-autoresearch]]
- Entities: [[wiki/entities/deep-research]]

## Core points

- The essay argues many engineering organizations lack clear visibility into true software delivery costs and returns.
- It frames AI-era development as an economics problem as much as a productivity problem.
- It questions whether traditional human-maintainability assumptions hold under agent-heavy development models.

## Why it matters

This source adds organization-level economic reasoning to a corpus otherwise centered on tools and implementation patterns.
