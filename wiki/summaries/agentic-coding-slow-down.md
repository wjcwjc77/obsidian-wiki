# Thoughts on Slowing Down Agentic Coding

- Source: [[raw/sources/blogs/Thoughts on slowing the fuck down|Thoughts on slowing the fuck down]]
- Topics: [[wiki/topics/deep-research-and-autoresearch]]
- Entities: [[wiki/entities/deep-research]]

## Core points

- The article critiques speed-first agentic coding and links it to brittle systems, low discipline, and growing quality issues.
- It argues for restoring engineering guardrails: explicit design decisions, review discipline, and bounded autonomy.
- It distinguishes where agents help most versus where human judgment should remain primary.

## Why it matters

It provides a counterweight to acceleration narratives and is useful for governance and quality conversations.
