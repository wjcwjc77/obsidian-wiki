# Agent Engineering

## Role in this corpus

Agent engineering is the broad bucket for how agents are structured, provisioned, observed, and operated beyond the base model itself. In this corpus it follows `raw/sources/agent/engnieering/` and anchors material on managed agents, runtime decomposition, and operational design around long-running systems.
