# Deep Research

## Role in this corpus

Deep research is the broad bucket for agents and workflows that perform extended investigation, prediction, or iterative self-improvement over longer horizons. In this corpus it follows `raw/sources/agent/deepresearch/` and groups both benchmark-oriented research agents and reusable research loops.
